Project Overview & Executive Summary

Healthcare organizations generate massive volumes of critical operational and clinical data across patient admissions, laboratory diagnoses, emergency room visits, and bed utilization. Converting this complex healthcare data into life-saving operational intelligence requires more than writing ad-hoc SQL queries—it demands a robust, production-grade automated analytics engineering workflow.

This comprehensive production-level project demonstrates an end-to-end modern data engineering and business intelligence platform built from scratch. By implementing a Medallion Data Lakehouse Architecture (Bronze → Silver → Gold), the platform automates raw clinical data ingestion, data transformations, rigorous quality testing, and interactive multi-departmental visualization.

Core Analytical Questions Solved:

  • 1. Clinical & Patient Flow: What is the average patient length of stay (ALOS), and which clinical departments experience the highest readmission rates within 30 days?
  • 2. Operational Resource Allocation: How is ICU bed utilization trending daily, what are emergency room waiting times, and how should hospital management optimize bed turnover during peak admission surges?
  • 3. Disease & Diagnostic Patterns: Which ICD-10 clinical diagnoses represent the highest patient volume and mortality risks across different demographic segments?

Modern Tech Stack & Architecture:

  • PostgreSQL & MinIO S3 (Data Lakehouse): Serves as the robust analytical data warehouse and object storage data lake for staging synthetic and realistic healthcare records (simulating MIMIC-IV and clinical EHR events).
  • dbt (Data Build Tool): Powers the transformation layer, modeling data from Bronze raw tables into cleaned Silver staging views and business-ready Gold star-schema data marts (`fct_admissions`, `mart_readmission_rates`, `mart_department_workload`, `mart_clinical_diagnoses`) with 100% automated schema and data quality testing.
  • Apache Airflow: Orchestrates the entire ETL workflow, executing scheduled DAGs that manage data ingestion, dependency resolution, transformation triggers, and pipeline monitoring.
  • Python & Pandas: Drives automated synthetic clinical data generation (`load_bronze_layer.py`), generating realistic departments, 600+ patient profiles, 2,100+ admissions, and 5,200+ diagnoses with complex readmission tracking.
  • Metabase BI: Delivers self-service interactive analytics dashboards tailored for Hospital Executives, Clinical Directors, and Operational Managers.

1. Medallion Data Architecture & Orchestration Pipeline

The platform is engineered around the industry-standard Medallion Architecture, ensuring strict data governance, auditability, and analytical performance across three progressive tiers:

  • 🥉 Bronze Layer (Raw Ingestion): Ingests raw CSV data and simulated EHR clinical records directly into MinIO S3 buckets and PostgreSQL raw staging schemas without modification, preserving the true historical state of patient events.
  • 🥈 Silver Layer (Cleaned & Staged): Built via dbt models, this layer deduplicates patient records, standardizes timestamps, normalizes diagnostic codes, and enforces relational integrity between patients, visits, and clinical encounters.
  • 🥇 Gold Layer (Business Data Marts): Highly optimized dimensional fact and dimension tables organized in a star schema. These data marts pre-calculate critical hospital KPIs (readmission rates, bed occupancy, ALOS) for zero-latency dashboard queries.

2. Interactive Analytics Dashboards & Key Insights

To serve diverse stakeholders across the healthcare facility, the visualization layer is partitioned into three specialized interactive BI dashboards in Metabase:

A. Executive Dashboard (Macro Hospital Health)

Designed for Chief Medical Officers and C-suite leadership, providing immediate real-time visibility into top-line facility metrics:

  • Total Patient Admissions: Tracking over 3,842+ cumulative admissions with month-over-month growth variance analysis.
  • Average Length of Stay (ALOS): Benchmarking facility-wide ALOS at 4.2 Days, enabling capacity planning and cost optimization.
  • 30-Day Readmission Rate: Monitoring readmissions (currently tracking at ~14.2%) to evaluate clinical efficacy and post-discharge care protocols.
  • ICU Bed Utilization: Real-time gauge tracking critical care bed occupancy (averaging 87.4%), alerting management when utilization exceeds safe threshold limits.
Figure 3: Metabase Executive and Operational Analytics Dashboards displaying real-time hospital KPIs.

B. Operations Dashboard (Workflow & Efficiency)

Empowers department managers and nursing supervisors to optimize daily patient flow and resource utilization:

  • Department Workload Analysis: Visualizing admission volumes across General Medicine, Emergency Department (ED), Surgery, and Intensive Care Units (ICU).
  • Emergency Waiting Times: Measuring patient intake bottlenecks with real-time tracking of triage-to-bed wait times (averaging ~31 minutes during peak hours).
  • Bed Occupancy Heatmaps: Identifying department-specific capacity crunches to enable proactive staff re-allocation and patient transfer management.
Figure 4: Metabase patient flow tracking from admission type (Emergency, Urgent, Elective) to hospital department.

C. Clinical Dashboard (Patient Outcomes & Diagnostics)

Provides epidemiological and clinical insights for medical research teams and quality control committees:

  • Primary ICD-10 Diagnoses: Categorizing over 5,200+ diagnostic events to identify prevalent health conditions and seasonal infection surges.
  • Mortality & Outcome Tracking: Correlating patient age, treatment duration, and ICU admission history with recovery rates and clinical outcomes.
  • Readmission Root-Cause Analysis: Isolating specific diagnostic categories and treatment protocols associated with elevated 30-day readmission risk.
Figure 5: Metabase SQL analytical query exploring average treatment cost and patient volume across insurance types in gold.fct_admissions.

3. Production Deployment & Data Governance

A core differentiator of this platform is its adherence to software engineering and DevOps best practices for data platforms:

  • Containerized Infrastructure: The entire stack (PostgreSQL, MinIO S3, dbt, Apache Airflow, Metabase) is fully containerized using Docker Compose, allowing instant zero-cost local reproduction and seamless cloud deployment.
  • Automated Data Quality Testing: Built-in dbt tests enforce not-null constraints, unique primary keys, valid relational foreign keys, and accepted value ranges across all Bronze, Silver, and Gold tables before dashboard publication.
  • Version-Controlled Transformation Lineage: Every SQL transformation and schema definition is managed in Git, providing complete auditability and lineage tracking from raw source files to executive charts.
